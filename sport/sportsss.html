The Importance of Sports

Sports play an integral role in promoting physical and mental health, strengthening community ties, and fostering a sense of pride and achievement in Pura Nazir. The local sports culture encourages youth to stay active and engaged, developing discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills that benefit both individual participants and the entire village.
