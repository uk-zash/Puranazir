Welcome to Madarsa Mohmmadiya Islamiya

Located in the heart of Pura Nazir, Madarsa Mohmmadiya Islamiya is dedicated to nurturing the minds and hearts of children in our community. Established with the vision to provide quality education, our school aims to instill values, discipline, and knowledge in the younger generation.

Mission

Our mission is to nurture the minds of young children by providing a comprehensive, inclusive education while promoting moral values. We aim to equip our students not only with academic knowledge but also with the wisdom and character that will guide them throughout their lives. Our goal is to build a generation of responsible and compassionate individuals.

Affiliation

Madarsa Mohmmadiya Islamiya is an independent educational institution offering education up to the 5th grade. We focus on providing a strong foundation for our students, preparing them for future academic pursuits.

Admission Information

Admissions for new students are open at the beginning of every academic year. For the academic year 2025-2026, we are accepting students for grades 1 to 5.

  • Eligibility: Students between the ages of 5 and 10 years old are eligible for admission.
  • Process: Parents can fill out an online application form or visit the school to collect a hard copy.
  • Fee Structure: The fees vary depending on the grade level. Please contact the school office for detailed information.
